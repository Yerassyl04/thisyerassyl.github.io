SamrukAI is a full-stack investment analytics platform for exploring stocks, running
machine-learning price forecasts, and constructing scientifically optimized portfolios.

[View on GitHub](https://github.com/Yerassyl04/SamrukAI)

**Highlights**

- Market explorer and per-stock detail pages with OHLCV charts, company info, and ticker news.
- ML prediction panel comparing ElasticNet, Random Forest, and Linear Regression with forecast overlays.
- Portfolio builder with Black–Litterman optimization, Ledoit–Wolf shrinkage, and an efficient-frontier lab.
- Context-aware AI assistant (Groq, Llama 3.3 70B) that interprets ML and optimization results.

**Stack:** React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, scikit-learn, SciPy, Docker.
